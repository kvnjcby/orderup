OrderUp!

Team Members:

  • Kevin Jacoby (kvnjcby)
  • Cameron McCullers (cameross)

OrderUp is a kitchen order management system. Wait staff can create food orders and add food items to them. Kitchen staff can then view orders, accept them, and mark them completed.

Technologies used:

  • JavaScript (jQuery, Bootstrap, typeahead.js)
  • PHP
  • MySQL
  • Bower
  • Grunt
