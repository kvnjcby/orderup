OrderUp!

Team Members:

  • Kevin Jacoby (kvnjcby)
  • Cameron McCullers (cameross)

OrderUp is a kitchen order management system. Customers can select food, place food orders and "pay" for them. Kitchen staff can then view orders, accept them, and mark them as completed.

Technologies used:

  • JavaScript (Node.js, restify, jQuery, Bootstrap, typeahead.js)
  • MongoDB
  • Bower
  • Grunt
